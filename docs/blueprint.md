# **App Name**: Elegance Aesthetics

## Core Features:

- Appointment Form: Appointment scheduling form with date, time, and service selection.
- Image Gallery: Elegant image carousel to showcase the salon's work in nails, hair, microblading, and aesthetic treatments.
- Service List: Display of services offered with a short description of each.

## Style Guidelines:

- Dark background (#121212) for a sophisticated and modern look.
- Use of gold (#FFD700) as an accent color to convey luxury and elegance.
- Neutral shades (whites and grays) for text and secondary elements to ensure readability against the dark background.
- Clean and minimalist layout with plenty of negative space to enhance the feeling of luxury.
- Use of simple, elegant icons to represent services and navigation elements.
- Subtle transitions and animations to add a touch of sophistication without being distracting.
